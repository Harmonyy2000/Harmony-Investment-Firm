Funds

Harmony Growth Fund

The Harmony Growth Fund focuses on long-term capital appreciation by investing in companies with high growth potential across various industries. This fund seeks to outperform market benchmarks by identifying innovative and promising opportunities.

Global Income ETF

The Global Income ETF provides investors with exposure to a diversified portfolio of income-generating assets from around the world. This ETF aims to deliver consistent income through investments in bonds, dividend-paying stocks, and other fixed-income securities.

Tech Titans Fund

The Tech Titans Fund focuses on high-risk, high-reward investments in leading technology companies. With a concentrated portfolio of innovative giants, this fund aims to capture the growth potential of the tech sector while acknowledging its inherent volatility.

Environment Impact ETF

The Environmental Impact ETF seeks to align investments with sustainability goals by targeting companies with strong environmental practices and positive societal impact. This ETF invests in eco-friendly industries, promoting both financial returns and positive change.

Healthcare Innovators Fund

The Healthcare Innovators Fund focuses on investments in companies at the forefront of medical breakthroughs and healthcare advancements. This fund aims to capitalize on the evolving healthcare landscape, potentially offering growth opportunities while managing risks.
